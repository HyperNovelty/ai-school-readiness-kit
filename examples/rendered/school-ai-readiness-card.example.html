Synthetic Review Card: Draft Feedback For Middle School Writing

Review status: ready_for_local_review

Summary

Card ID
synthetic-middle-writing-feedback-001
School context
A synthetic grade 7 language arts classroom reviewing whether AI may help draft teacher feedback on practice paragraphs.
Audience
Teacher, students, families, and local program lead
AI use case
The teacher may use AI to suggest possible feedback language for low-stakes practice writing, then review and edit every comment before students see it.
Student data involved
Yes
Family communication needed
Yes
Assessment impact
No grades, placement, discipline, or official records are changed by the AI output.

Equity and Access Notes

  • Students can complete the assignment without using AI.
  • Feedback should not assume paid tools, home broadband, or a specific device.
  • The teacher checks comments for language clarity and bias before sharing.

Human Review Steps

  • Teacher removes names and unnecessary details before drafting feedback.
  • Teacher reviews every AI-suggested comment before students receive it.
  • Teacher stops the workflow if comments are inaccurate, harsh, or confusing.

Not For

  • Not for grading
  • Not for discipline
  • Not for placement
  • Not for detecting whether a student used AI
