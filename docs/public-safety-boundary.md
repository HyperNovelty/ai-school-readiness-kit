# Public Safety Boundary

This kit is a local readiness aid. It is not a legal, compliance, medical, or mental-health tool. It does not authorize policy choices and does not decide whether a school system may adopt a vendor or platform.

Do not use the kit to store or process real student data. Do not add names, student IDs, grades, disability records, disciplinary records, health information, family contact information, private school incidents, or account credentials.

The safest use is to describe proposed AI use in general terms, review the likely effects, and decide what local human review is needed before anything touches students.

If a proposed use involves sensitive records, assessment consequences, surveillance, counseling, health, discipline, or legally regulated processes, mark it `needs_policy_review` or `do_not_use` and use the appropriate local process.
