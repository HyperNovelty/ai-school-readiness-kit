# Open Lab Positioning

`ai-school-readiness-kit` is the school and learning-program readiness repo in the Hypernovelty Open Lab public footprint. It helps reviewers turn a proposed AI classroom or learning-program use into a concrete readiness card with privacy, assessment, equity, family communication, and human-review notes.

It connects with:

- `hypernovelty-survival-kit` for the umbrella map and education-readiness survival cards.
- `hypernovelty-verification-literacy-kit` for classroom-friendly verification labs.
- `source-card-schema` for public-safe source and evidence notes.
- `ai-workflow-safety-screen` for general AI workflow screening.
- `agent-run-receipts` for documenting agent-assisted changes to school-facing materials.

## Public-Safe Boundary

This kit is a local review aid, not legal, compliance, medical, mental-health, student-safety, procurement, or policy advice. Public examples must stay synthetic. Do not store student data, names, grades, disability records, disciplinary records, account details, school incidents, or credentials in this repo.
