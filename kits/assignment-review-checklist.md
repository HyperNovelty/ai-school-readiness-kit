# Assignment Review Checklist

Use this checklist before AI changes an assignment, rubric, feedback process, or student submission rule.

- The assignment states whether AI help is allowed, limited, or not allowed.
- The allowed uses are specific enough for students to follow.
- The teacher can evaluate the work without relying on unverifiable AI-detection claims.
- The assignment does not reward students mainly for having better paid tools.
- Students have a way to ask for clarification before submitting work.
- Feedback generated or drafted by AI is reviewed by a human before reaching students.
- Any assessment impact is described plainly.
- The assignment has a non-AI path that still lets students meet the learning goal.

Mark the card `needs_policy_review` if the use changes grading rules, academic integrity handling, placement, or official records.
