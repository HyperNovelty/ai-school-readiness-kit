# Student Privacy Checklist

Use this checklist before any AI use involves student information.

- No real student data is placed in local examples or public materials.
- The proposed use describes the minimum information needed.
- The review identifies whether names, grades, writing samples, behavior records, disability information, health information, or family information could be exposed.
- A human reviewer checks whether synthetic or anonymized examples can be used instead.
- Family communication is planned when student data is involved.
- The use is stopped or escalated if the reviewer cannot explain where data goes.
- The card includes at least one human review step.

If student data is involved, the readiness card must set `family_communication_needed` to true and include human review steps.
